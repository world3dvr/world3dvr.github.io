Epic the musical

1. Modern Twist on Classic Mythology Retells The Odyssey in a fresh, emotional, and epic way. Features characters like Odysseus, Poseidon, and Circe in a relatable, dramatic light.

2. Powerful Music & Lyrics Songs like “Hero Too,” “You,re Mine,” and “Full Sails” are deeply emotional and cinematic. Combines orchestral grandeur with modern storytelling and lyrics. Jorge Rivera-Herrans (creator/composer) is compared to Lin-Manuel Miranda for his writing talent.

3. Relatable Themes Deals with identity, family, war trauma, loss, and perseverance. Odysseus is portrayed as a flawed, complex hero struggling to find home — emotionally and literally

4. Amazing Vocal Performances Broadway-level singing from artists like: Corey Cott (Bandstand, Newsies) Teal Wicks (Wicked, The Cher Show) James D. Gish, Ethan Carlson, and more.

5. Visually Rich Storytelling (Coming Soon) Currently audio-only (like a musical podcast), but there's talk of a full-stage production or animated adaptation in the future. The concept art and fan animations are already varry common

6. Growing Fandom There's a massive community making animations, covers, and fan art. The community is supportive and creative — great to be a part of.

7. It's Still Unfolding Only a few arcs (episodes) are out, so you can catch up easily. New content is in progress, and the creator actively involves fans in the journey.
